The Shop

About The Shop

Elaine Kedai Bunga Dan Cenderahati (宜霖花艺礼品屋) is a flower shop which open by Auntie Elaine which her main goal is to show her talents on designing, crafting, and packaging flowers into different type of style. The shop is located at Bandar Mahkota Cheras, Selangor and there are total of 3 people who worked there which are Miss Elaine, her husband or her son as helper.

The Story of the owner

Miss Elaine like to show her talents on designing, crafting, and packaging flowers into different type of style. She sell bouquet flower depends on which events, activities, or seasonal holiday the customer needed for. Events are such as open ceremony, valentine day, mother day, birthday, and graduation while seasonal holiday such as Easter Day, Christmas Day, or Qingming Festival. Her mission is to make beautiful and high-quality flowers for clients to give to their beloved one such as spouse, girlfriend, family, or friends. Her target is to spread love, kind, respect, and honour to their beloved one, bring down fragrances and colours to their daily life.
